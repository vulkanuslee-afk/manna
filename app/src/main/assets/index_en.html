🙏 Welcome to Daily Manna

This app is a Scripture meditation tool — hand-picked Bible verses organized by season of life (work, family, anxiety, comfort, and more), shown one at a time.

Which verse you receive is random, but it is not meant as a special sign or oracle. Like manna in the wilderness, the hope is simply that one verse a day finds a home in your heart.
